About Freshdew Children Clinic

For over a decade, we've been dedicated to providing exceptional pediatric care to children in Owerri and surrounding communities. Our passion is your child's health.

Our Journey

Where Compassion Meets Pediatric Excellence

Founded in 2012 by Dr. Amina Okoro, Freshdew Children Clinic began as a small community clinic with a big vision: to transform pediatric healthcare in Owerri. What started as a modest practice has grown into a comprehensive pediatric care center serving thousands of families annually.

Our name "Freshdew" symbolizes renewal, purity, and gentle care—qualities we bring to every child's health journey. We believe every child deserves access to world-class medical care delivered with warmth and compassion.

12+ Years of Service
15,000+ Children Treated
98% Parent Satisfaction

Our Mission

To provide exceptional, comprehensive pediatric healthcare that addresses the physical, emotional, and developmental needs of every child, while supporting families through education, compassion, and partnership.

We are committed to delivering evidence-based medical care in a child-friendly environment that promotes healing, comfort, and trust.

Our Vision

To be the leading pediatric care provider in Southeast Nigeria, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative care models, and unwavering commitment to child health advocacy and community wellness.

We envision a future where every child in our community has access to quality healthcare that supports their growth into healthy, thriving adults.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every interaction we have with our patients and their families.

Compassionate Care

We treat every child as if they were our own, with kindness, empathy, and genuine concern for their wellbeing.

Clinical Excellence

We maintain the highest medical standards through continuous learning, evidence-based practice, and advanced technology.

Family Partnership

We believe in collaborating with families as essential partners in their child's healthcare journey.

Integrity & Trust

We build relationships on honesty, transparency, and respect, earning the trust of families through consistent, ethical care.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and approaches that enhance patient outcomes and improve the care experience.

Community Commitment

We actively contribute to community health through outreach programs, education, and accessible care initiatives.
