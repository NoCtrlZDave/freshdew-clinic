Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us and our services.

What are your clinic hours?
Our regular clinic hours are Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Sundays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We offer 24/7 emergency services for urgent pediatric cases.
Do I need an appointment?
While appointments are recommended to minimize waiting time, we do accept walk-in patients. However, appointments receive priority scheduling. For same-day appointments, please call us at +234 123 456 7891.
What should I bring to my child's first appointment?
Please bring your child's medical records, immunization card, any current medications, your insurance information (if applicable), and a list of any allergies or medical conditions. Arrive 15 minutes early to complete registration paperwork.
Do you accept health insurance?
Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including NHIS, and private insurance providers. Please contact our billing department at billing@freshdew.com or +234 123 456 7892 to verify your coverage before your visit.
Is parking available at your clinic?
Yes, we have ample free parking available for patients and visitors. Our parking lot is located at the rear of the building with designated spaces for patients with disabilities.
